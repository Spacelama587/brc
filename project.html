Prospect Park Dash

The Prospect Park Dash is a family-friendly run in the heart of Brooklyn's iconic Prospect Park, encouraging kids and parents to enjoy an active day together in nature.

This event is designed to foster community spirit and fitness for all ages. Participants can enjoy scenic park views and engaging activities before and after the race. Join us to celebrate health, family, and community!
